About Us

Fitness Kulture was established in 2019 with the aim of providing top-notch fitness solutions for individuals who are passionate about health and well-being. We offer a variety of fitness options, including yoga, crossfit, body care, and martial arts classes like kickboxing and cardio kickboxing, among many others.

Business Highlights

- Open Every Day: We are open every day of the week, including Sundays from 5:30 AM to 11:00 PM.

- Wide Range of Amenities: Our gym is equipped with state-of-the-art cardio and strength training equipment, sauna, and more.

- Fitness Options: We offer personalized fitness plans and group fitness classes.

Our Mission

At Fitness Kulture, we focus on creating a welcoming and motivating environment for everyone—from beginners to seasoned athletes. Our professional trainers ensure that every member receives personalized guidance, whether they’re engaging in group classes or one-on-one training.
